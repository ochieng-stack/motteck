Cookie Policy

Learn how Motteck uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, improve performance, and enhance browsing experiences.

How We Use Cookies

Motteck uses cookies to understand website traffic, remember settings, improve functionality, and provide a better user experience.

Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core website functions.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand visitor activity and improve the website.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember settings such as language or device preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used by advertising partners to show relevant ads.

Third-Party Cookies

Some trusted third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers may place cookies through our website.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
